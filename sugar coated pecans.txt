Sugar coated pecans

Ingredients:
1 cup sugar
3/4 table spoon salt
1/2 table spoon cinnamon
1 egg white
1 tablespoon water

Instructions:
- preheat oven to 250 degrees
- in a mixing bowl add sugar, salt, and cinnamon and whisk until frothy
- pour one pound of nuts into liquid. stir until all nuts are covered in liquid 
- dump coated nuts into cinnamon / sugar mixture and mix until all the sugar is gone from the bottom of the bowl 
- prepare two cookie sheets with baking paper or spray
- spread nuts over the two cookie sheets and bake for one hour
- every 15 minutes mix nuts 
- let cool once done
