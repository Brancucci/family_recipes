chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups butter
1 1/4 cups sugar
1 1/4 cups packed brown sugar
1 table spoon vanilla
4 cups all purpose flour
2 table spoons  baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 bag (24 oz) chocolate chips

Instructions:
- heat oven to 350 degrees
- in large bowl, beat butter, sugar, vanilla, and eggs on medium speed until light and fluffy
- stir in flour, baking soda, and salt (dough will be stiff)
- stir in chocolate chips 
- on ungreased cookie sheet, drop dough by table spoon 2 inches apart. Flatten slightly
- bake 11-13 minutes until light brown 
- cool 1-2 minutes and remove from cookie sheet 
- ** optional ** stir in chopped nuts when adding the chocolate chips
