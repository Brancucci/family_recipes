Banana Bread

Ingredients:
1 1/4 cups sugar
1/2 cup butter
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups ripe bananas 
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 1/2 cups flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup chopped nuts

Instructions:
- heat oven to 350 degrees
- grease one 9"x5" loaf pan
- in a large bowl, mix sugar and butter.
- Add eggs, bananas, buttermilk, and vanilla then beat with electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
- stir in flour, baking soda, salt, and nuts just until moistened
- pour into pan and bake for 1 1/4 hours until passes the toothpick test
- let cool for 10 minutes
