pecan balls

Ingredients:
1/2 cup unsalted butter softened
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup pecans
1/4 tablespoon salt
* optional 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions:
- heat oven to 275 degrees
- cream together the butter, sugar, and flour with a mixer
- fold in the pecans and salt until combined. The mixture will be crumbly and will stick to itself
- roll the dough into small balls (1/2 inch) and place on ungreased cookie sheet. 
- bake for 45 minutes
- let cool completely on cookie sheet before removing. *if you touch or move the balls before cooling, they will fall apart.
- add your favorite toppings such as melting the chocolate chips and drizzling on top.
