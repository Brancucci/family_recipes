Chocolate cake

Ingredients:
1 (4 oz) package of Bakers German sweet chocolate
1/2 cup boiling water
1 cup butter
2 cups sugar
4 eggs separated (use yolk and white in separate instances)
1 (15 oz) coconut pecan frosting
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 cups all purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon 
1 cup buttermilk

Instructions:
- heat oven to 350 degrees
- melt chocolate in a frying pan with the water then cool
- mix butter and sugar by hand
- beat in egg yolks
- stir in vanilla and chocolate
- mix in flour, baking soda, and salt
- beat egg whites until stiff peaks form. fold into batter
- pour batter into cake pan
- bake for 30 minutes or passes toothpick test
- cool for 15 minutes then frost
